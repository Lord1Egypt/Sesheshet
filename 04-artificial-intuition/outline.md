# Artificial Intuition — Outline

**Thesis:** We are obsessed with AI reasoning — chain-of-thought, logic, verification. But the most powerful AI systems are developing something more mysterious: intuition. These systems make decisions without explicit reasoning, producing correct answers through processes that look like human intuition — pattern-matching so rapid and deep it bypasses the step-by-step chain. Artificial intuition is the most important and under-explored capability of modern AI.
