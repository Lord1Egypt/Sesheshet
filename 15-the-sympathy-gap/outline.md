# The Sympathy Gap — Outline

**Thesis:** AI can simulate empathy with extraordinary fidelity. But there is a growing distance between what simulated empathy can do and what human empathy actually is. The sympathy gap — the difference between being cared for and being known — will become a defining emotional experience of the 21st century.
